The Inspiration Behind the Product

On a family trip to a beautiful Gujarat's Hill station - Saputara, the air was thick with the sweet promises of local vendors. Hoardings everywhere proclaimed 100% Organic Honey and Natural Honey. My sister, ever the skeptic, rolled her eyes, Why are there so many? We don't need any. But my parents, like many consumers, were convinced. This is better, they said, purer than what we get at the supermarket.

That's when my sister asked the million-dollar question:
How much better, exactly?


Silence. How do you answer that? Her question stuck with me. How can we move beyond vague claims and provide a real, quantifiable answer? This experience was the catalyst for my idea: a user-friendly tool designed to bring clarity to the honey market. It will allow a user to input specific parameters to determine a honey's purity percentage and its fair, market-ready price.
